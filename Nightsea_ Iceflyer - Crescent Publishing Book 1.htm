Nightsea: Iceflyer

Price: $17.99 (Hardcover), $6.99 (Paperback)

The tale begins with Carrik's first vision under the aurora-filled skies of Yeth, setting him on a path to unite a world. "Iceflyer" introduces readers to the icy planet of Yeth and the mysteries that Carrik will unravel in his quest to unite the human clans and the enigmatic Yeth'l. This seminal work sets the stage for the epic Nightsea series, offering a blend of science fiction and fantasy that captures the imagination and inspires wonder.

"Iceflyer sets a new standard for exploratory fiction, blending mythic elements with a raw depiction of survival against the cosmos. Carun's prose is both poetic and powerful, making every page a compelling journey." — Lisa Thompson, Galaxy Gazette
