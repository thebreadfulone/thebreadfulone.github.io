About the Author

"Carun isn't just spinning tales; he's weaving entire universes. 'Nightsea' is his tapestry, rich with the fabric of a reality reimagined." — Jordan Michaels, The Daily Explore
"In a genre that has seen it all, Carun redefines wonder. His 'Nightsea' saga is an epic journey that maps the human soul against a cosmos teeming with the fantastical." — Alex Reed, Ocean Mysteries Magazine
"Roger Carun, the cartographer of the impossible, charts a course where stars and psyche collide. His 'Nightsea' plunges into depths unseen, an odyssey not just through space, but the human condition." — Casey Donovan, Adventure Reads Weekly

Roger Carun, celebrated author of the "Nightsea" series, weaves intricate tales that blend swashbuckling adventure, space opera, and hard sci-fi into novels that not only entertain but provoke thought. With a background in marine biology, Carun's passion for exploration—both terrestrial and cosmic—shines through in his writing. His books explore the complex dynamics of societal evolution, the permanence of change, and the human condition across the cosmos.

After spending a transformative year alone on a remote island, Carun returned not just with the framework for 'Nightsea' but with a renewed vision for storytelling. This experience deepened his narratives, infusing them with a philosophical undertone that challenges readers to explore their own depths. His works are praised for their richly developed worlds, complex characters, and the meticulous integration of science with fantastical elements, setting him apart as a visionary in science fiction.

'Nightsea' is more than just a series—it's a voyage that Carun invites his readers to embark upon, promising encounters with the mystical and the mysterious, all anchored in the realities of science. Each book serves as a chapter in an ongoing exploration of what it means to venture into the unknown, making Carun not only a storyteller but a chronicler of human spirit and interstellar adventure.
